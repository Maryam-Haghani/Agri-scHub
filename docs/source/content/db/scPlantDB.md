# scPlantDB

- **Description:**  A plant-focused scRNA-seq database curating 67 datasets from 367 experiments across 17 species, covering 2,546,778 cells, 259 cell types, and 229,551 cell markers for streamlined exploration and comparison of plant single-cell data.
- **Latest Version:** v1.1
- **Release Date:** 2023-7-10
- **Link to Website:** [https://biobigdata.nju.edu.cn/scplantdb](https://biobigdata.nju.edu.cn/scplantdb)
